Short bio

I am a lecturer in Information Systems at Universitas Ma Chung. My work focuses on the design, development, and evaluation of Health Information Systems (HIS), with particular interests in clinical decision support systems, knowledge management in healthcare, and applied data-driven solutions for improving healthcare services and organizational decision-making.

My academic background includes a Master’s degree in Computer Science (Institut Pertanian Bogor) and a Bachelor’s degree in Information Systems (STTI NIIT I-Tech). I also have extensive experience in web and software development, educational technology, and community-oriented capacity building.
