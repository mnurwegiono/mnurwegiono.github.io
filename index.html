Muhammad Nurwegiono

Lecturer, Universitas Ma Chung, Indonesia

Hi, I’m Muhammad Nurwegiono. I am a lecturer in the Information Systems program at Universitas Ma Chung. I also work as a Web Developer at TissuePro Technology in Florida, USA.

My research focuses on the design and development of Health Information Systems, particularly the integration of decision support systems, knowledge management, and data-driven technologies to improve healthcare services and clinical decision-making.

Research interests: Health Information Systems, Clinical Decision Support Systems, Health Data Analytics, Knowledge Management in Healthcare, and Applied Information Systems.
